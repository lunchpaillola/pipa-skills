# Grow Relationships Examples

## Client Check-In

- `Relationship`: client sponsor
- `Why now`: milestone slipped and no update went out this week
- `Suggested touch`: short status note with blocker, owner, and next date
- `Owner`: account lead or `TBD`
- `Timing`: today
- `Source`: delivery notes
- `Watchlist`: trust risk if next date slips again

## Reminder Wording

`follow up with client` stays here. `remind me by email next Friday` routes to `pipa-follow-up-reminders`.
