# Pipa Deliver Work

Use when active delivery needs coordination, status, blockers cleared, risk mgmt, triage, or momentum.

## Starter Jobs

- Produce current-state update.
- Find blockers, owner gaps, stale work, deps, escalations.
- Triage tickets/client requests/signals into now vs later.
- Reframe old `execute`/`monitor`/`status`/`triage` as public `deliver work`.

## Required Inputs

- Source: tasks, issues, notes, status thread, plan, or `TBD`.
- Horizon: today, week, milestone, launch, or `TBD`.
- Owners, dates, status, and evidence where available.

## Source Methods

- `references/execute-work-package-coordination.md`: owner/action/date/status.
- `references/execute-iteration-cycle.md`: cycle rhythm.
- `references/execute-dependency-and-handoff.md`: handoffs + deps.
- `references/monitor-status.md`: leadership status.
- `references/monitor-ticket-triage.md`: intake/ticket sorting.
- `references/monitor-risk-escalation.md`: blockers + escalation.

## Connected Capabilities

- `pipa-triggers`: only automate future updates/event reactions.
- `pipa-huddle-beta`: only live voice delivery talk-throughs.
- `composio`: only explicit live app reads/writes.

## Workflow

1. State objective, horizon, sources.
2. Summarize current state and what changed.
3. Identify blockers, risks, owner gaps, stale handoffs.
4. Recommend smallest next action per item.
5. Flag next lane: `get paid`, `grow relationships`, or `learn from the work`.

## Output Shape

- `Current state`: short summary.
- `What needs attention`: blockers, risks, stale items, or `none found`.
- `Actions`: item, owner, next action, due/review date, status, evidence/source.
- `Escalation trigger`: condition and owner, or `TBD`.
- `Unknowns`: unresolved details as `TBD`.
- `Follow-ups`: minimal asks.

## Rules

- Do not imply work is done without acceptance evidence.
- Do not create automations from recurring wording until trigger workflow confirms proposal.
- Do not route money health/client relationship risk away unless that intent dominates.
